Your medicine. Your choices. Made simpler.

We’re two students on a mission — helping people find affordable medicine alternatives by uncovering what’s hidden behind brand names and overpriced prescriptions.

At MediCare, we help users find trusted, affordable alternatives to high-cost branded medicines — often cutting prices from thousands to just a few rupees. Backed by real-time inventory and research, our platform ensures smarter, transparent choices in healthcare. We're actively building AI-powered tools to improve recommendations, and soon integrating expert-verified data from certified doctors. Our mission is simple: make quality medicine accessible, credible, and fair for everyone — no compromises.
